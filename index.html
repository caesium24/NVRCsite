    About Us

    New Venture Research Corporation (NVRC) is a fast growing Information Technology Services Company composed of professionals representing a wide array of technical competencies with the sole focus of serving our clients and helping them achieve effective and secure solutions.

    NVRC supports the engineering needs of a variety of customers, but specializes in Department of Defense work for Joint, Navy Expeditionary and Mine Warfare systems.

    Beyond experience, NVRC employees also carry industry certifications associated with their areas of expertise

    Innovation

    At NVRC, our mission is to provide services to our customers that achieve not only the original objective but also create future proof approaches to tackling prospective goals.
